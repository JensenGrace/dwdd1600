About Me

Driven by Design

My name is Grace E. Jensen and I am a current student in the arts. Although, my relationship with art began long before my formal education. I’ve loved creating for as long as I can remember, and every stroke, line, click of a shutter, and design, is a reflection of how I see the world.

On this website, you’ll find a collection of my best works in the Gallery. If something catches your eye, visit the Purchase page to own a finished piece, or head to the Commision page to collaborate on something bespoke.

Whether you’re here to explore, shop, or connect, thanks for popping by. I look forward to collabarating with you, Enjoy.
