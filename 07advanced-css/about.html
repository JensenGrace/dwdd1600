About Me

Driven by Design

My name is Grace Jensen, although I sign all of my fine artwork Grace E. Viitala, after my grandmothers maiden name. Iâ€™ve loved creating for as long as I can remember, and cannot imagine my life without it.

I was born and raised in Utah and have been drawing and painting for as long as I can remember. I am continually inspired by the creative people around me, always pushing me further in my own work. I have learned so much and am so greatful to all my amazing teachers and peers that have helped me get to where I am now. I am currently working towards my BFA degree through Utah Valley University, graduating Fall 2026. Outside of art I spend most of my time playing tennis, hiking, and pursuing other creative hobbies.

My general process includes five steps from beginning. I begin with research. This can look different project to project. Sometimes it takes a lot of searching, other times inspiration finds me. Next step in the process is always sketching. After I have put the idea pen to paper the next and most important step is production and final touches. Depending on the project this can take anywhere from 5 to 100 hours, usually returning many times. The very last thing I do is getting the artwork where it needs to go.
