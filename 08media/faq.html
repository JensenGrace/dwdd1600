Frequently Asked Questions

Artwork

Q.A.
What mediums do you work with?I use a variety of methods and materials, but traditionally I stick with classic oil on canvas.
Do you sell originals?Yes, I do. If you are interested in buying original contact me directly.
Is your work on display?Yes, I am freequently in and out of various shows, galleries, and exhibitions.
How do I see your work?I post updates on my About Me page, and my social media about where to find my current works on display.

Commisions

Q.A.
Do you accept commissions?Yes, with stipulation. I do not accept everything, I go on a project by project basis.
What kind of commissions do you take?I am always accepting bottles and mini oil painting commissions. Other commissions are accepted on a case by case basis only.
How do I request a commission.Reach out to me through the "Contact" page on this website, or on social media.
How much do commissions cost?Bottles are $300+shipping, Minis are $20+shipping. Every other request will vary on price.
