Declaration of Independence

What it Says

We hold these truths to be self-evident, that all men are created equal, that they are endowed by thier Creatoro with certain unalienable Rights, that among these are Life, Liberty and the pursuit of Happiness...

In every stage of these Oppression we have petitioned for redress in the most humble terms: Our repeated petitions have been answered only by repeated history...
