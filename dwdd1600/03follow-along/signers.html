Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of INdependence include

Thomas Jefferson
Thomas Jefferson, a delegate from Virginia, was the principle author of the Declaration of Independence.
John Adams
John Adams, representing Massachusetts, was a vocal advocate for independence in teh Continental Congress.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the United States and is remembered for their courage in signing the Declaration.
